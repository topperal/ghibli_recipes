Calcifer`s Breakfast

Ingredients:

  • 3 thickly cut bacon slices
  • 6 eggs
  • 1 tsp of thyme
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Sourdough bread

Tags:

breakfast

easy

less 30 min

3 portions

meat

Return to all recipes ⏪

Instructions:

  1. Heat the frying pan on low heat and fry the bacon until golden brown.
  2. Move the bacon to a corner of the pan and fry 6 eggs on low heat as well.
  3. Serve when the eggs are fried to your preferred doneness.
  4. Season with thyme and salt and fry for 1 minute.
  5. Serve together with the eggs, bacon and sourdough bread.
  6. Now you can eat it! Itadakimasu!
